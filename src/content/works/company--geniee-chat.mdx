## Responsibilities and Highlights

- Led the frontend refactoring and technology stack selection for the management dashboard, building the project from scratch designed. Built a SPA with **Vite** enhancing development speed and efficiency through fast hot-reloading and optimized bundling for a seamless front-end experience.
- Developed backend APIs and BFF ( Backend For Frontend ) services to implement key business features, ensuring smooth data integration and efficient logic execution.
- Designed and implemented a node-based workflow system with drag-and-drop functionality, streamlining business process orchestration and simplifying product configuration for client-specific needs. Collaborated with cross-functional teams to refine requirements, integrate user feedback, and enhance product features, leading to successful adoption by internal and external clients.
